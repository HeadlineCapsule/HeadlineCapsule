He began accumulating his 2.7 million shares in mid-March and also acquired call options expiring between June 2024 and January 2025 for another 210,000 shares at a $2 strike price. Ramaswamy intends to engage with BuzzFeed’s Board of Directors and management about operational and strategic opportunities to maximize shareholder value.
